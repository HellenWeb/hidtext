<div id="mp-tfa" class="mp-contains-float"><div id="mp-tfa-img" style="float: left; margin: 0.5em 0.9em 0.4em 0em;">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 140px;">
<a href="/wiki/File:Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_(cropped).jpg" class="image" title="Kalki Koechlin"><img alt="Kalki Koechlin" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/30/Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg/140px-Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="140" height="186" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/30/Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg/210px-Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/30/Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg/280px-Kalki_Koechlin_unveils_Nokia_Lumia_820_%26_920_mobile_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="551" data-file-height="734" /></a><div class="thumbcaption" style="padding: 0.25em 0; word-wrap: break-word;">Kalki Koechlin</div></div>
</div>
<p><i><b><a href="/wiki/Margarita_with_a_Straw" title="Margarita with a Straw">Margarita with a Straw</a></b></i> is a 2014 Indian <a href="/wiki/Hindi" title="Hindi">Hindi</a>-language <a href="/wiki/Drama_(film_and_television)" title="Drama (film and television)">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Shonali_Bose" title="Shonali Bose">Shonali Bose</a>. It stars <a href="/wiki/Kalki_Koechlin" title="Kalki Koechlin">Kalki Koechlin</a> <i>(pictured)</i> as an Indian teenager with <a href="/wiki/Cerebral_palsy" title="Cerebral palsy">cerebral palsy</a> who relocates to America for her undergraduate education and <a href="/wiki/Coming_of_age" title="Coming of age">comes of age</a> following her complex relationship with a <a href="/wiki/Visual_impairment" title="Visual impairment">blind</a> girl, played by <a href="/wiki/Sayani_Gupta" title="Sayani Gupta">Sayani Gupta</a>. <a href="/wiki/Revathi" title="Revathi">Revathi</a>, Kuljeet Singh, and <a href="/wiki/William_Moseley_(actor)" title="William Moseley (actor)">William Moseley</a> play supporting roles. Produced by Bose in partnership with <a href="/wiki/Viacom18_Studios" title="Viacom18 Studios">Viacom18 Motion Pictures</a>, <i>Margarita with a Straw</i> was co-written by Bose and Nilesh Maniyar. The film deals with the concepts of <a href="/wiki/Human_sexuality" title="Human sexuality">sexuality</a>, <a href="/wiki/Inclusion_(disability_rights)" title="Inclusion (disability rights)">inclusion</a>, <a href="/wiki/Self-love" title="Self-love">self-love</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Self-acceptance" title="Self-acceptance">self-acceptance</a>. Bose conceived the idea for the film in January&#160;2011, during a conversation with <a href="/wiki/Malini_Chib" title="Malini Chib">Malini Chib</a>—her cousin and a <a href="/wiki/List_of_disability_rights_activists" title="List of disability rights activists">disability rights activist</a>—about the latter's desire to have a normal <a href="/wiki/Sex_life" title="Sex life">sex life</a>. After winning <a href="/wiki/Sundance_Institute" title="Sundance Institute">Sundance Institute</a>'s Mahindra Global Filmmaker Award for the draft, she modified the script to reflect her own perspective, incorporating several personal experiences into the narrative. (<b><a href="/wiki/Margarita_with_a_Straw" title="Margarita with a Straw">Full&#160;article...</a></b>)
</p>

<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/New_Mexico_Territory" title="New Mexico Territory">New Mexico Territory</a></b> was an <a href="/wiki/Historical_regions_of_the_United_States" title="Historical regions of the United States">organized incorporated territory of the United States</a> from September&#160;9, 1850, until January&#160;6, 1912.  This illustration, created by Henry Mitchell for <i><a href="/wiki/Historical_armorial_of_U.S._states_from_1876#State_Arms_of_the_Union" title="Historical armorial of U.S. states from 1876">State Arms of the Union</a></i>, published by <a href="/wiki/Louis_Prang" title="Louis Prang">Louis Prang</a> in 1876, depicts the territory's coat of arms, adopted by legislation in 1887: "The coat of arms of the territory of New Mexico shall be the Mexican Eagle grasping a serpent in its beak, the cactus in its talons, shielded by the American eagle with outspread wings, and grasping arrows in its talons." The year 1850, representing the territory's organization, appears in <a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">Roman numerals</a> under the eagles, and below the shield is the Latin motto <span title="Latin-language text"><i lang="la">Crescit eundo</i></span> ('It grows as it goes').
